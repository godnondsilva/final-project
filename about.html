About Us:

Phone Commerce is a website which aims to help users get rid of their old and unused phones or buy a new or used phone.
It will provide a way for those users who wish to sell their phones either to some person or recycle it so that it can be taken care of naturally.
It will also provide a way for those users who wish to buy phones at a discounted rate. Pre-owned phones will also be provided
PhoneCommerce is the eBay of mobile phones!
